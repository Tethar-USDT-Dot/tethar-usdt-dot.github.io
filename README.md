## Abstract

**Tethar USDT• (USDT Dot)** is a stablecoin designed to maintain a stable value at parity with the US dollar (USD). Deployed on the BNB Smart Chain (BSC) as a BEP-20 token, USDT• combines the stability of reserve-backed assets with the efficiency of blockchain technology.

This whitepaper outlines the vision, mechanics, tokenomics, and roadmap for USDT•, emphasizing its role in providing a reliable digital dollar alternative for payments, DeFi integrations, and cross-border remittances.

## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Overview
Tethar USDT• addresses the need for stability in the cryptocurrency market by providing a reliable digital asset pegged to the value of the US dollar. Unlike volatile cryptocurrencies, USDT• is designed for everyday use, allowing users to hold, transfer, and transact without the risk of value erosion.

The token’s smart contract is verified on the BNB Smart Chain, incorporating standard features for minting, burning, and transfers, making it suitable for both retail and institutional use.

### 1.2 Mission and Vision
* **Our Mission:** To provide a trustworthy stablecoin that bridges traditional finance with decentralized systems, reducing volatility and enhancing liquidity.
* **Our Vision:** A global economy where users can utilize digital dollars seamlessly, promoting financial inclusion and innovation in decentralized finance (DeFi) and everyday payments.

## 2. Market Context

### 2.1 The Problem
* **Volatility:** Traditional cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum suffer from high price volatility, making them unsuitable for savings or usage as a standard unit of account.
* **High Fees:** Existing stablecoins on networks like Ethereum often suffer from prohibitive gas fees, making small transactions inefficient.
* **Centralization Risks:** Many stablecoins rely heavily on opaque centralized issuers, creating trust barriers for users.

### 2.2 The Solution: Tethar USDT•
USDT• fills the gap by offering a stable, compliant, and user-friendly token on the scalable **BNB Smart Chain**.
* **Low Fees:** Benefiting from BSC’s efficiency, transactions cost pennies and settle in seconds.
* **Transparency:** Designed for clear on-chain supply visibility.
* **Stability:** Aims for a 1:1 USD peg via reserve-backed issuance and market mechanisms.

---

## 3. Key Features & Technology

### 3.1 Algorithmic & Reserve Stability
Tethar USDT• employs a hybrid approach to stability. It utilizes reserve-backed minting (where tokens are minted against deposited assets) alongside algorithmic adjustments to manage token supply dynamically based on market demand.

### 3.2 Security & Interoperability
* **Verified Contract:** The smart contract code is verified and open-source (MIT license).
* **BEP-20 Standard:** Fully compatible with Metamask, Trust Wallet, and all BSC DeFi applications.
* **Admin Controls:** Includes industry-standard security features such as pause, blacklist, and adminTransfer to prevent illicit activity and ensure regulatory compliance.

### 3.3 Environmental Sustainability
We are committed to sustainability by operating on the BNB Smart Chain, which uses a Proof-of-Staked-Authority (PoSA) consensus mechanism. This creates a significantly lower carbon footprint compared to Proof-of-Work chains.
